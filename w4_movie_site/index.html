About the Movie

Mid90s is a coming-of-age comedy-drama film written and directed by Jonah Hill in his directorial debut. The film stars Sunny Suljic as Stevie, a 13-year old boy living in Los Angeles in the mid-1990s. Stevie navigates a turbulent home life while finding camaraderie and purpose among a group of skateboarders at a local skate shop. The film explores themes of adolescence, friendship, and the search for identity against the backdrop of 1990s skate culture. It received critical acclaim for its authentic portrayal of youth and the skateboarding scene, as well as for Hill's direction and Suljic's performance.

Release Date: October 19th, 2018

Director & Writer: Jonah Hill

Genre: Comedy, Drama

Runtime: 85 minutes

Rating: R
